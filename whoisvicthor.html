📌 Who am I?

November 03rd, 2024

Hey there!

My name is Victhor Sartório. I was born in Espírito Santo, a small and relatively unknown state in Brazil, but have been living in the city of Rio de Janeiro for about half of my life now, which is currently clocking in at a bit over 29 years!

I found out about programming in my first year of High School at 14 because of some RPG making software that used some variation of Ruby, and then got into C, Python and a salad of other stuff at different points in the following years.

At the time of choosing what to study in University, I went with Statistics after suffering a lot between a bunch of completely unrelated options like the good little nerd that I was (everything seemed fun). In Uni, I fell in love with stats and decided to follow an academic career, which I gave up by the end of my MSc due to realizing maybe industry was a better fit for approach to solving problems.

Ever since, it’s been about 5 years of industry experience in Data Science, and although every once in a blue moon I do think about just straight up software engineering, I just enjoy stats and ML too much to go full software. I won’t get into details though, because this isn’t a CV.
